About Our Chama

AKL Beyond Borders Group is a premier financial community founded by visionary individuals dedicated to transforming lives through collective savings and strategic investments.

We have grown from a small savings group to a robust financial ecosystem with over 100 members across different regions, all united by a common goal: financial freedom.

  • Transparent operations and governance
  • Member-centric approach
  • Proven track record of returns
  • Strong community support network
